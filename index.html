How to use 'To-Do' App?
In order to use this add your todo item's title in the place where it says 'To Do Title' and then choose the desired date from the datepicker. Finally click on the button "Add Item".

If you want to mark an item as finished click on 'Completed'.
If you want to revert an item back to its unfinished state click on 'Undo'.
If you want to remove an item simply click on 'Remove'.

Enjoy.
